Brownies

Description

Making brownies can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 cup coca powder
  • 1 cup chococlate chips
  • 2 large eggs

Method

  1. Combines all dry ingredients
  2. Add wet ingredients and mix
  3. Add mixture to a lined baking sheet
  4. Whack in the oven at 350F for 1 hour
  5. Tuck in
