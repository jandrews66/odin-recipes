Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1lb Ground Pork
  • 1lb Lasagna Pasta
  • 1 Jar Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Jar Bechemal Sauce

Method

  1. Add oil to pan and brown the meat
  2. Meanwhile cook pasta in boiling water per packet instruction, then strain
  3. In a rimmed dish, layer meat, then red sauce, then white sauce, then pasta, repeat.
  4. Whack in the oven at 350F for 1 hour
  5. Tuck in
